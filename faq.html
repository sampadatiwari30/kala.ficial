Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kala.ficial, our services, and how to make the most of your artistic journey.

Kala.ficial is a global platform for emerging artists to showcase their artwork, collaborate with other creators, and connect with exhibitions worldwide. We empower artists and enrich visitor experiences through immersive virtual tours, artist spotlights, and comprehensive exhibition information.

To showcase your artwork on Kala.ficial, you need to create an artist account. Once registered, you can upload high-quality images of your artwork, add descriptions, set prices, and manage your portfolio. Our team will review your submission to ensure it meets our quality standards before it goes live.

Purchasing artwork is simple. Browse our collection, select the piece you love, and click "Purchase." You'll be guided through a secure checkout process. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Once purchased, we'll coordinate shipping directly with you.

Yes! We regularly organize both physical and virtual exhibitions. As a registered artist, you'll receive notifications about upcoming exhibition opportunities. You can submit your work for consideration, and if selected, we'll guide you through the participation process.

Creating an artist profile and browsing artwork is free. We charge a commission only when artwork is sold through our platform. Exhibition participation may have separate fees depending on the event. All fees are transparently displayed before any transaction.

We're committed to supporting emerging artists through:

  • Visibility through our platform and social media channels
  • Exhibition opportunities both online and offline
  • Educational resources and workshops
  • Networking events with galleries and collectors
  • Fair commission structures that prioritize artist earnings

We welcome diverse art forms including:

  • Paintings (oil, acrylic, watercolor, etc.)
  • Sculptures and installations
  • Photography and digital art
  • Mixed media artworks
  • Prints and limited editions
  • Textile and fiber art

All artwork must be original creations by the submitting artist.
