Ananda Das

Ananda Das, a scion of artistic royalty from the vibrant heart of Calcutta, emerges as a beacon of contemporary Indian artistry. The son of the distinguished Lakshmi Narayan Das, Ananda's aesthetic voyage began under the tutelage of his father, whose acumen as a consummate commercial artist ignited the nascent genius within him.

Shiv Kumar Soni

Shiv Kumar's work primarily focuses on reminiscing his childhood times. He elaborately expresses the simple joys of those days that lie in moments with any kind of engagement. He marvels at the innocence that is deeply rooted in those formative days.

Priyendra Shuka

As the moment flashes by, leaving its color on my being, I try to hold on to it. For these colors fade away with time, pushed back into oblivion by fresh colors of new moments. Such are the workings of the ever changing panorama that is life. I long to internalize the moment and bring it to the realm of the tangible, where I can go back and live it again.
