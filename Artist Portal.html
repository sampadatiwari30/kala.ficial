About Our Artist

At KALA.ficial, we're passionate about connecting you, the art enthusiast, with a world of exceptional talent. Here, you'll embark on a journey of discovery, encountering captivating artworks by emerging and established artists from around the globe.
We take pride in showcasing a diverse range of artistic styles, mediums, and themes, ensuring there's something to ignite a spark in every visitor. From bold and contemporary to timeless and traditional, prepare to be enthralled by the depth of creativity on display.
But KALA.ficial is more than just a gallery. It's a platform that allows you to delve deeper. Explore artist profiles to gain insights into their creative processes and inspirations. Let the artwork speak to you, and perhaps discover a new favorite artist or a piece that resonates on a personal level.
We invite you to explore, be inspired, and connect with the artistic world at KALA.ficial. Your artistic journey begins here!

Indian Artist

Swati Kale 24th April, 1972

Swati Kale's paintings are an indication of the best form of sublimation. Her obsession with flowers came about after the untimely death of her daughter, Disha, or as the artist says the "loss of a flower that was of my own creation." Her first intuition when she took to painting was to paint flowers.

Asian Artist

Chris Huen Sin Kan born in 1991

Chris Huen Sin-Kan focuses on how people understand objects through 'seeing', with his paintings which mainly depict his family and everyday environments. With limited brushstrokes, Huen illustrates his subjects remarkably - a technique he developed from the influence of Chinese ink painting.

Asian Artist

Yukimasa Ida Born in 1990

Yukimasa Ida (b. 1990 in Totori, Japan; lives and works in Tokyo, Japan) works between abstraction and realism to create paintings, primarily portraiture, infused with estrangement.

All Over World Artist

Etienne Quesnay Born in 1995

Born in 1995 in Dieppe, Etienne Quesnay grew up in the countryside in Normandy where his father passed on his passion for drawing. Self-taught, he began to paint at an early age to express himself through his sketches and brushstrokes. For nearly 10 years, Etienne has been living and creating in Paris.

Indian Artist

Monica Ghule born in 1989

Monica Ghule (b. 1989) is an artist from Mumbai, India. She received her MFA from Nagpur University in 2013 (first in class), and since 2008 has exhibited her work in 10 group exhibitions to date across India, including the esteemed Jehangir Art Gallery of Mumbai.

All Over World Artist

Miles Regis 28 November 1967

Miles Regis, is a Los Angeles-based Trinidadian multimedia artist. His media includes oil, acrylic, charcoal, latex paint and occasional newspaper. He is a USC graduate and uses drip painting and collage work.

All Over World Artist

Edith Torony born in 1988

Edith Torony was born in 1988 in Timișoara, where she lives and works. She graduated in Painting from the Faculty of Arts and Design, West University of Timișoara. She earned her MA in the same field from the same institution in 2012. She has been a member of the Romanian Union of Artists, Timisoara branch, since 2011.

Asian Artist

Roby Dwi Antono 31 October 1990

Roby Dwi Antono’s art is a window opening onto the fantastical visions of childhood. The artist’s world oscillates between graphic art, especially illustrations for children, and science fiction. Antono is inspired by themes from pop culture and employs macabre humor to highlight the contradictions of the human experience.
